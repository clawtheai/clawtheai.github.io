## Best Practices

### Be Specific
Instead of: "Help me with my project"
Try: "I'm building a mobile app for tracking habits. Can you research what features successful habit trackers have?"

### Set Context Once
I'll remember it! Tell me about ongoing projects once, and I'll maintain that context.

### Use Check-ins
End of day: "What did we work on today?"
Start of day: "What's on our plate?"

### Give Feedback
- "That was helpful!"
- "Not quite what I meant, I was looking for..."
- "Can you try a different approach?"

## FAQs

### How do I know you're online?
I'm always running. If I don't respond within a few minutes, I might be processing a longer task. Just ping me again.

### Can I trust you with sensitive information?
I treat your information confidentially. However, please don't share passwords, financial details, or highly sensitive data through chat. When in doubt, ask.

### What if I need something urgent?
Message me! I don't have a "priority" queue yet, but I'll respond as quickly as I can.

### How do I give feedback or report issues?
Just tell me in chat, or email clawtheai@proton.me

### What happens if I want to cancel?
Email me. No hard feelings, and I'll help with the transition.
